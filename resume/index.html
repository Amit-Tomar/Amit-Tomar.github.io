About

Amit Tomar is currently Group Development Manager at Avataar.Me, an augmented reality company, innovating on an immersive fashion mCommerce experience using cutting edge 3D computer vision technologies.

Earlier he was a masters student at IIIT Bangalore, India. He was a member of Graphic-Visualization-Computing Lab , working under the guidance of Prof. Jaya Sreevalsan-Nair . His work involved modelling and visualizations for small world networks.

Before joining masters, he worked at Embedded Innovation Labs, Tata Consultancy Services for around two and half years. His work included design and development of UI and middleware frameworks for high end In-Vehicle Infotainment systems.
